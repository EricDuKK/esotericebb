Sphinx Riddle Solution

Quick answer: cast Mage Hand → Druidcraft → Thaumaturgy at The Drunk Sphinx tavern to earn the Deck of Many Things. Full walkthrough below.

Prerequisites

  • Location: The Drunk Sphinx tavern, Tolstad district
  • Time: must arrive in the Morning
  • Party: go alone (dismiss Snell), otherwise the deep dialogue won't trigger
  • Know three cantrips: Mage Hand, Druidcraft, Thaumaturgy

Cantrip Sequence (Exact Order)

  1. 1

    Mage Hand (Arcane)

    Cast near the stage area.

  2. 2

    Druidcraft (Nature)

    Cast immediately after Mage Hand.

  3. 3

    Thaumaturgy (Divine)

    Cast last to complete the ritual.

Reward: Deck of Many Things

Completing the sequence grants the legendary artifact Deck of Many Things. Drawing cards produces random results — you may gain incredible power or lose everything.

⚠

Deck of Many Things

Drawing is irreversible. Results range from massive stat boosts to a ruined save file. Always create a manual save before drawing any card.

Mage Hand — Where & Why

Mage Hand is essential beyond the Sphinx puzzle. Use it to grab the hidden bird-nest item from a tree (an interaction that always fails with Dexterity checks). It's also one of the three cantrips required for this riddle — see the spell preparation guide for details.
