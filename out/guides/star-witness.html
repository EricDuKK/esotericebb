Star Witness — Day 3 Walkthrough

Snell keeps telling you to explore City Below but nothing happens? Here's how to progress.

The Problem

On Day 3, Snell repeatedly reminds you to explore City Below, but the quest log doesn't update. This is the single most common stuck-point reported by the community.

Solution

  1. 1

    Head to City Below — Northwest

    There is a hidden door in the NW section of City Below that leads deeper underground.

  2. 2

    (Optional) Learn Detect Secret

    Visit the Goblin Underground Garden (landmark: central pool). Walk west and find the Underdwarf. Complete their task to learn the Detect Secret spell.

  3. 3

    Reveal the hidden entrance

    Cast Detect Secret (see spell preparation guide) in the NW corner to instantly reveal the entrance. Without the spell, you can still find it by pixel-hunting along the walls.

Navigation Tips

  • Press Journal to see all discovered doors and connections — the closest thing to a map.
  • Use Behold to scan for hidden shortcuts — it works as an environmental radar.

Point of No Return

⚠

Endgame Warning

Entering The Pillar triggers the endgame sequence and you cannot return. Before going in:
  • Confirm you have the Deck of Many Things (if desired).
  • Complete all companion side-quests.
  • Create a manual save named "Endgame_Backup".

Speak with Dead — Don't Bother on Day 1

Speak with Dead requires a level-3 spell slot (player level 5+). On Day 1 you won't have it. Even if you could cast it, the body is removed by elves as part of the story — the corpse disappearing is not a bug.
